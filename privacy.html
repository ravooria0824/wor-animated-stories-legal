Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026.

This Privacy Policy describes how the WOR Animated Stories application (the “App”) handles data when the operator authorizes it against their Google account. The App is operated for a single channel owner and does not provide accounts or services to third parties.

1. Scope

The App is an internal content production pipeline. It generates 2D animated POV story shorts and publishes them to the YouTube channel owned by the authorizing Google account. The App is not offered to the general public.

2. Information We Access

When the operator completes Google’s OAuth consent flow for the App, the App receives an OAuth refresh token and access tokens limited to the following scopes, used only against the authorizing account’s own YouTube data:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube.upload — upload videos to the operator’s channel.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/youtube — manage videos, playlists, and channel resources owned by the operator.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/yt-analytics.readonly — read YouTube Analytics for the operator’s channel to inform the content feedback loop.

The App does not request access to email, contacts, Drive, or any other Google data outside the above YouTube scopes.

3. How Information Is Used

OAuth tokens are used solely to (a) publish videos produced by the pipeline to the operator’s own YouTube channel, (b) manage video metadata and playlists on that channel, and (c) read aggregate analytics for the operator’s own channel to tune content scheduling and topics.

4. Storage and Security

The OAuth refresh token is stored encrypted at rest in Google Cloud Secret Manager within the operator’s own Google Cloud project (vidz-499720). Access is restricted by Google Cloud IAM to the operator and the pipeline service identity. The token never appears in application logs and is not transmitted to any third party.

5. Sharing

The App does not sell, rent, or share any information accessed through Google OAuth with any third party. Information accessed via the YouTube and YouTube Analytics APIs is used only by the operator’s own pipeline running in the operator’s own Google Cloud project.

6. Retention

OAuth tokens are retained until the operator revokes them at Google Account Permissions or until the App is rotated. On revocation or rotation, the prior credentials are deleted from Secret Manager.

7. Google API Services User Data Policy

The App’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

8. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated to reflect changes to the App or to applicable requirements. The current version always lives at this URL with the “Last updated” date above.
